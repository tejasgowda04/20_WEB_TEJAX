About Us

A Legacy of Academic Excellence

Founded in 1985, our college has been a beacon of higher education in the region for over four decades. We offer undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Science, Arts, Commerce, and Management disciplines.

Our NAAC A+ accredited institution is committed to fostering critical thinking, innovation, and holistic development. With world-class infrastructure, experienced faculty, and an active student community, we provide an environment where every student thrives.

Our industry-aligned curriculum, cutting-edge research labs, and strong alumni network spanning 30+ countries ensure that our graduates are globally competitive.
